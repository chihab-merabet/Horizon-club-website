What is Horiz​on Club?

Horizon Club, established in 2009, is a student club at ENSTTIC in Oran. We are passionate about shaping the future of technology and innovation through a wide range of activities and projects. Our club serves as a platform for students to explore, learn, and create. Join us at Horizon Club and be a part of our tech-driven journey as we shape the future of IT and ICT. there's a place for you here, and together, we'll reach new horizons!

Our main events

Welcome to Ideator, Horizon Club's premier competition in collaboration with ANPT and sponsored by Algérie Télécom. It's a three-stage innovation journey(workshops, pitches, and finals) where visionaries refine, pitch, and compete for the top spot. With ANPT's incubation support and Algérie Télécom's sponsorship, Ideator is your unique opportunity to turn innovative ideas into impactful realities. Join us and celebrate the power of ideas!
